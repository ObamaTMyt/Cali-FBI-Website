About Us

Federal Bureau of Investigations

FBI is a hidden gem in CaliRP. We maintain and uphold the standard of what it means to be a member of the LEO Community. Our Command Team is committed to bringing their absolute best and provide all of our Agents with the proper support and dedication they deserve. Our specially trained Agents are resilient, committed, and relentless in the pursuit of Justice. Our Department also is the most Active Main FED.

Why Should You Join FBI?

If you desire fulfillment and are interested in setting records and making history, FBI is the place for you. If you have integrity, honor, courage, and commitment, you would fit right in here. We pride ourselves on professionalism and our response to the call of justice.

FBI Extras

The FBI proudly offers a wide range of prestigious awards to recognize outstanding achievements, including the Medal of Honor for officers who go above and beyond for the department. These rewards can be presented at any time by department heads to celebrate exceptional dedication and performance. Additionally, our Agents have the opportunity to participate in exclusive Director Special Initiatives designed to inspire excellence and innovation.

Our Goal

Our main objective is to create a fun and enjoyable experience within CaliRp LEO and strive to be the best with constant updates and upgrades. These help us become more interactive within the community and CaliRp in general.
