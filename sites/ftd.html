Field Training Division Information

The FBI's Field Training Division (FTD) serves as the foundation for preparing the agency's finest operatives. Tasked with equipping agents with advanced skills and knowledge, FTD focuses on rigorous training programs that emphasize tactical precision, critical decision-making, and teamwork. As the gateway for agents entering the FBI's elite units, FTD ensures that every member meets the highest standards of excellence. Within FiveM, the Field Training Division stands out as a cornerstone of professionalism and discipline, helping agents transition from training to real-world operations. Their unmatched dedication to building highly skilled teams has earned them recognition as the backbone of the bureau's operational success. With FTD, every agent is set up to thrive in the dynamic and demanding environment of California Roleplay.
